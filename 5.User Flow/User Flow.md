## User Flow

**Userflow Explanation for Customer Role in a Nutrition Assistant Application:**

**1. User Registration & Profile Setup:**
* The customer opens the app and signs up using their details.
* During registration, they provide age, weight, height, gender, and activity level.
* After registration, they can access their profile and update these details anytime.

**2. Food Consumption Logging:**
* On the home screen or dashboard, the user sees an option to add food.
* They search for food items from a database.
* They select the item and input the quantity or serving size.
* This gets saved in their daily log or diary.

**3. Diet Plan Creation:**
* The user navigates to the "My Diet Plan" section.
* They can create a new plan by choosing their goals (e.g., weight loss, maintenance).
* They select start and end dates and preferred food types or restrictions.
* The plan is saved and tracked daily.

**4. Nutritional Information Review:**
* The app displays the nutritional breakdown of each logged item (calories, protein, carbs, fats).
* Users can review total daily intake or per-meal breakdown.
* This helps them understand what they are consuming.

**5. Following Recommendations:**
* Based on input data and goals, the app provides personalized tips or recommendations (e.g., increase protein intake).
* Users view this guidance on their dashboard or notification area.
